Virat Kolhi

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the India national team. He is a right-handed top-order batsman who also occasionally bowls right-arm medium pace. He plays for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League and Delhi in domestic cricket. Widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world, Kohli is the only cricketer to be named ICC Player of the Year multiple times, winning the award in 2012, 2017, and 2018. He has also won the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy (ICC Cricketer of the Year) and the ICC ODI Player of the Year award in 2017 and 2018.

Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is an Indian international cricketer who plays for the India national team and Chennai Super Kings in the Indian Premier League. He is a right-handed middle-order batsman and wicket-keeper. Widely regarded as one of the best finishers in limited-overs cricket, Dhoni is one of the most successful Indian captains across all formats of the game. Under his captaincy, India won the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cups, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy. He is the only captain to have won all three major ICC trophies. Dhoni is also regarded as one of the best wicket-keepers in the world and holds numerous records for his batting and wicket-keeping.

Bumrah

Jasprit Jasbirsingh Bumrah is an Indian international cricketer who plays for the India national team and Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League. He is a right-arm fast bowler known for his unique bowling action and ability to bowl yorkers at will. Bumrah made his international debut in January 2016 and quickly established himself as one of the best bowlers in the world across all formats of the game. He has been ranked among the top bowlers in both Test and One Day International (ODI) cricket by the International Cricket Council (ICC). Bumrah is known for his pace, accuracy, and ability to generate bounce, making him a formidable opponent for batsmen around the world.
